We’re climate positive

Being certified as Climate Positive means an organisation has gone beyond achieving “Net Zero” and instead creates an environmental benefit by removing additional greenhouse gases from the atmosphere, not just those that it emits.

Kind is a climate positive business and have taken the step to balance twice our greenhouse gas emissions since 2014 (when we were founded).

Our charity partnerships

Kind have been members of 1% for the Planet since 2017 which requires us to donate a minimum of 1% of our turnover to environmental causes.

In 2019 we selected Rewilding Britain as our environmental charity partner and pledged the majority of our 1% donations to their cause.
