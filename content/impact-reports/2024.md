  <div class="prose col-span-full lg:col-span-8 xl:col-span-6">

  As we approach our 10th birthday this summer, we’re reflecting on what keeps us going. That is the belief that creative work can be a genuine force for good.


  This year reinforced that belief. We gifted a complete brand strategy and identity to Base51, a local charity doing vital work with young people. Choosing to give that work freely, because we could see the difference it would make, reminded us why Kind exists.

  
  We certified our website as climate-positive and continued our 1% for the Planet commitment, donating over 25% of our profits to environmental charities. And we took over running People, Planet, Pint in Nottingham, creating space for others doing this work to connect over a drink and honest conversation.

  
  Looking ahead, we’re excited about deepening our relationships with existing clients and finding new ways to amplify the impact of organisations tackling the climate crisis and social inequality. We’re also keen to explore how we can better measure the real-world impact our work creates, moving beyond the work we create to understanding the change it helps achieve.

  
  We’re still learning, still evolving, and still committed to proving that you don’t need to compromise your values to build something that lasts.  </div>
